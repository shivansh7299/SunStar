About Us

SunStar is technology oriented , highly reputed , innovative and future focused global sourcing partner for high precision and quality Injection moulded components and Moulds. We are one stop complete solution provider ( provide services/solutions from Concept to Design to Moulds Manufacturing to Injection Moulding to Assembly ) company for world class products and services in Engineering and Plastics domain. We are having highly focused, knowledgeable and visionary leadership, strong domain expertise and state of the art manufacturing infrastructure at strategic locations at Pune INDIA to support the growth of our Indian and global clients. We are in business of providing the best in class solutions to our clients by adding the value at each stage thru our innovative and futuristic infrastructure, processes, products and value engineering solutions.We have most advanced, leading edge integrated infrastructure covering the entire spectrum of services from concept to mould design to mould manufacturing, injection moulding, decorating and assembling for a bouquet of industries including automotive, white goods, consumer durables, retail and engineering in India and abroad.
